# ME_CAM Customer Instruction Manual (v2.2.3+)

**Goal:** Get the camera online and secure in under 10 minutes.

---

## 1) Power On
1. Insert the SD card and connect the camera cable.
2. Plug in power.
3. Wait ~2 minutes for boot.

---

## 2) Connect From Your Computer or Phone
### Windows (Recommended)
1. Run: setup-domain-windows.bat (as Administrator)
2. Enter your camera’s IP address when prompted.
3. Open: https://ME_CAM.com:8080

### Mac / Linux
1. Add a hosts entry:
   - /etc/hosts → add: <YOUR_PI_IP>   ME_CAM.com
2. Open: https://ME_CAM.com:8080

---

## 3) First Login (One-Time Admin)
1. Sign in with the temporary admin credentials provided by your installer.
2. You will be prompted to create your **customer account**.
3. After creating your customer account, the **admin account is automatically removed**.

✅ From this point forward, **only your customer account can log in**.

---

## 4) Daily Use
- Dashboard shows live video and storage status.
- Motion events are logged automatically.
- Use Settings to adjust motion sensitivity and retention.

---

## 5) If You Forget Your Password
Contact support. The device will need a secure reset.

---

## 6) Quick Troubleshooting
- **Can’t open dashboard:** confirm power, then retry https://ME_CAM.com:8080
- **No video:** power cycle the camera, wait 2 minutes, refresh the page
- **Slow video:** reduce resolution or framerate in Settings
